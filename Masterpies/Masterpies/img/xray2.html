The X-ray Clinic in Aarhus C

At the RøntgenKlinikken, regular X-ray examinations and ultrasound scans are carried out. We have an agreement with the public health insurance. Therefore, it is free for patients in Region Midt to be examined after a referral from a doctor associated with the Swedish Health Insurance.

Outside of the public system, an appointment can be made for an X-ray and ultrasound examination, either for self-payment or via health insurance (a referral must be available, however).

With four incoming telephone lines and four secretaries to answer the phone, we are well equipped to answer incoming calls.

The clinic has two X-ray rooms and two ultrasound rooms. Our equipment is particularly high-quality and radiation-saving.

We employ five radiographers, all trained and licensed, like the medical secretaries.

The clinic is led by specialist Christian Ancher Iversen.

Failure to appear

If a patient without reasonable cause fails to cancel from an agreed time no later than the day before, the patient is obliged to pay the Røntgenklinikken a fee for no-show.

The fee is DKK 250 for failure to attend an examination (Agreement between Danish Regions and the Association of Specialist Doctors, 2015 §25 subsection 2).

What can we do?

At the RøntgenKlinikken, more than 45,000 examinations are carried out per year with state-of-the-art and particularly high-quality X-ray equipment from the most recognized suppliers.

All examinations are performed with radiation-conserving techniques. The quality of the equipment allows us to stay well below the required minimum radiation doses in the Danish Health Authority's regulations. Regarding the risk of X-ray examination, see the separate section "About X-rays" .
